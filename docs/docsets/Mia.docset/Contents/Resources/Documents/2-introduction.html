Introduction

The Nets Easy checkout provides a one-click checkout that handles payment methods, consumers shipping information, and stores this in a safe and secure manner. We have optimized the checkout to convert visitors to consumers by reducing the actions a potential buyer must do before completing a purchase.

The checkout offers everything through one integration – get access to payment methods, consumers information and their stored payment methods through one fast integration.
